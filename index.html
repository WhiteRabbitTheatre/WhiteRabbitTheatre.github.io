About White Rabbit Theatre

A Glastonbury-based community theatre for adults 18 and over. We produce full-scale productions, fundraisers, and variety shows throughout the year. Our fun, efficient, and extremely positive environment are all geared towards developing your skills as a actor, singer and dancer. White Rabbit Theatre's core mission is providing a forum for people to reach their full potential and share their talents with a live audience. Our cast and crew works to bring as entertaining a show as possible to Hartford County audiences. White Rabbit Theatre was started in 2015.

Interested in Joining?

White Rabbit Theatre is always looking for new actors, singers and dancers who enjoy theatre as much as we do. If you're interested in learning how to audition or to learn more about our organization, email WhiteRabbitTheatre.co@gmail.com
